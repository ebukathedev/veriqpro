See VeriQPro in Action

From product registration to consumer verification - our seamless process ensures authenticity at every step.

1

Product Registration

Unilever Nigeria registers products in the VeriQPro system with manufacturing details, batch numbers, and expiration dates.

2

QR Code Generation

The system generates unique, encrypted QR codes for each product unit with tamper-proof security features.

3

Product Distribution

Products with VeriQPro QR codes are distributed to retail outlets across Nigeria, ready for consumer purchase.

4

Consumer Verification

Customers scan the QR code using our web interface to verify product authenticity and earn rewards.

5

Verification Results

Instant verification shows product details and authenticity status, while rewarding customers with points.
